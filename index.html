Reflection

The entire Industry Visit Tour was a meaningful and memorable experience for me. Throughout the eight days, I was able to visit different companies, explore new places, and gain a better understanding of what to expect in the real-world workplace. Each day brought new lessons, whether from company visits, travel experiences, or simple moments with my classmates.

One of the things I truly appreciated was the accommodation provided by DJM Tours and Services Inc. The staff were kind and approachable, and they made sure that we were well taken care of throughout the trip. The place was clean and comfortable, and the meals more than enough for all of us, which made our stay even more amazing. Because of this, I felt safe and at ease during the entire tour.

This experience also helped me reflect on my own readiness for the future. There were moments when I realized that I still have a lot to learn, and although I have not fully regained the spark that I once had, this tour gave me a sense of hope. During college, I experienced burnout, and at times, I lost interest in what I was doing. However, this experience reminded me that I can still find my way back and continue improving step by step.

Overall, the tour was worth it. It gave me not only knowledge and experience, but also memories that I will carry with me. It was a journey that allowed me to learn, reflect, and appreciate both my present and my future path.

Places visited include: Hytech Power Inc., OpenText, MMDA, Teleperformance, Top Peg Animation, MicroSourcing, PMA, The Mansion, Baguio, Tagaytay

First Flight and First Steps into Manila

April 5, 2026 Manila

Summary of Activities

I arrived early at the airport and waited for our departure to Manila. While waiting, I met a few of my friends who were on the same flight. We had a smooth flight, and we arrived safely at NAIA a few minutes earlier than expected. After that, we waited for the bus that would take us to the DJM Dorm.

When we arrived at the dorm, we were given time to organize our things and settle in before lunch. After lunch, we started our city tour at around 1:15 PM through a bus provided by DJM Tours and Services Inc. During the bus ride, the tour guide shared information about the places that we passed, and she continued different discussions when we arrived at each destination.

We first visited Luneta Park, where we saw important historical landmarks such as the Rizal Monument and the execution sites of Rizal and Gomburza. After that, we went to Intramuros, where we explored Manila Cathedral and Fort Santiago. I felt amazed at Fort Santiago because I had only learned about it from textbooks before, especially the Spanish Coat of Arms, which was previously discussed by my high school teacher. I also saw a kalesa up close for the first time.

Before returning to the Dorm, we passed by SM Mall of Asia, and we were given time to explore the area. After some time, the bus picked us up and brought us back to the dorm. We then rested, had dinner, and ended the day with enough sleep.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

This was my first time riding an airplane, and I learned the proper procedures at the airport such as check-in, security inspection, and boarding. I also learned basic information about several historical places in Manila through the tour guide. In addition, I learned how actual historical sites provide deeper understanding compared to reading from textbooks alone.

Personal Reflection

The first day of the tour was meaningful because it allowed me to experience history in a more personal way. I felt a sense of appreciation as I stood in places that I had only read about before. I also felt excited to explore more locations in the following days. This experience helped me value both learning and travel as part of my personal growth.

Exploring Companies and Facing the Unexpected

April 6, 2026 Hytech Power Inc., OpenText

Summary of Activities

I woke up early to have breakfast and prepare for the day. At 8:00 AM, we rode a bus to Hytech Power Inc., a company that provides technical training and engineering services related to power systems. The employees gave us a tour, where they showed the machines used for training and introduced their training hub.

We then returned to the dorm for lunch. At 12:38 PM, we traveled again to OpenText, a company that offers solutions for managing digital information and business processes. The speakers explained their services and shared that employees can receive training and change roles within the company. They also prepared games with prizes and provided snacks.

After the visits, we went back to the dorm to rest and have dinner. Later, we were allowed to go outside as long as we followed the rules. My friends and I went to SM North EDSA, where we enjoyed activities such as Sky Glide and Gootopia. However, on our way back, we had difficulty finding transportation because there were limited Public Utility Vehicles, and we were not familiar with the routes. We felt worried, but we still managed to return on time, although we paid a higher fare. After that, I took a bath and slept.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned how companies like Hytech Power Inc. and OpenText train their employees and support career growth. I also realized that companies invest in people to improve their skills. At the same time, I learned the importance of being prepared and aware when traveling in unfamiliar places.

Personal Reflection

The second day of the tour was both educational and challenging. I enjoyed learning about how companies operate, but the experience outside the dorm made me realize how important it is to stay alert and responsible. Even though we felt worried at one point, we were still able to manage the situation, and that made the experience more meaningful for me.

Learning Systems and Taking Time to Rest

April 7, 2026 MMDA, TP

Summary of Activities

I woke up early, had breakfast, and prepared for the day. At around 8:07 AM, we rode a bus to our first destination.

We arrived at the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA), an agency responsible for traffic management and urban services in Metro Manila. A speaker explained their functions and discussed how they monitor traffic through AI systems and CCTV cameras. We were also given the opportunity to ask questions, which helped us understand their operations better.

After the visit, we returned to the dorm to rest and have lunch. At around 1:19 PM, we traveled again to Teleperformance, a company that provides customer service and business process outsourcing solutions. They explained employee benefits such as partner discounts, and they showed us their workspaces. We also noticed that there were many applicants, which showed the demand for jobs in the company.

Before returning to the dorm, we were given time to explore TriNoma, where we walked around and relaxed after the company visit. After that, we went back to the dorm earlier than expected, so my friends and I planned to go out later that evening. However, we felt very tired, and we ended up falling asleep instead.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned how agencies like MMDA manage traffic and maintain order in busy areas through technology and monitoring systems. I also gained an idea of how companies like Teleperformance operate and support their employees. In addition, I realized the importance of balancing activities and rest during busy schedules.

Personal Reflection

The third day was both informative and relaxing. I appreciated learning about how traffic systems work and how companies manage their operations. At the same time, I realized that rest is also important during trips like this. Even though we planned to go out, choosing to rest helped me recover for the next day’s activities.

DAY 04
Day 04 of 08

Inspiration, Reality, and Self-Reflection

April 8, 2026 Top Peg Animation, MicroSourcing

Summary of Activities

I woke up early, had breakfast with the group, and prepared for the day. At around 9:06 AM, we rode a bus to Top Peg Animation, a company that produces animation content for television and other media. They introduced their company, showed how they create animations, and shared some of their projects. They also allowed us to try drawing in their workspace, which made the visit more engaging.

At around 11:50 AM, we had a packed lunch because we could not return to the dorm on time. After that, we traveled to MicroSourcing, a company that provides outsourcing services and various job opportunities. The visit included several speakers who discussed the company, in-demand jobs, and their personal experiences, and they also provided food during the session.

This was the longest visit, and for me, it was the most impactful. The speakers inspired me and helped me understand what to expect in the working environment. It made me realize that I am not fully prepared yet, but I still have time to improve. This experience motivated me because I have felt burned out during college, and at times, I lost interest in my field.

After the visit, we returned to the dorm, had dinner, and went to a nearby laundry shop. Later, my friends and I went to a Hong Kong surplus store, where we bought clothes at low prices, and we also visited 7-Eleven. We then picked up our laundry, returned to the dorm on time, washed up, tracked my expenses, and went to sleep.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned how animation and outsourcing companies operate and offer different career opportunities. I also gained insight into in-demand jobs and realized the importance of preparing myself while I still have time.

Personal Reflection

The fourth day was the most meaningful for me. The speakers at MicroSourcing made me reflect on my current situation. Although I am not fully ready for the real world, I felt motivated to improve and regain my interest in my field. This day reminded me that I still have time to grow.

DAY 05
Day 05 of 08

A Fun Experience in Tagaytay

April 9, 2026 Tagaytay

Summary of Activities

This day was a holiday, so we did not have company visits. Instead, we went out to relax and enjoy the day. At around 6:20 AM, we rode a bus to Tagaytay People’s Park, where we explored the area, took pictures, and bought souvenirs.

After that, we traveled again at around 9:10 AM, and packed lunches were given during the ride. My friend and I decided to eat early while on the bus.

We then arrived at Sky Ranch Tagaytay, where we spent several hours enjoying the rides. I felt scared during the Super Vikings ride with the class, and many of us were shouting, which made it a memorable experience. We also tried other rides such as the Mini Vikings, bumper cars, go-kart, roller coaster, and Ferris wheel. I also spent time with a friend at Cartoonville, where we tried the slide and took pictures. It felt like a break after the previous company visits.

At around 2:00 PM, we returned to the dorm so the bus could be prepared for the trip to Baguio the next day. My friends and I planned to go out again, but we were not allowed due to an issue. We stayed in the dorm, had dinner, washed up, and prepared to sleep. I felt disappointed because our plans were canceled, but I still chose to rest.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned the importance of taking breaks and enjoying time with others, especially after busy activities. I also realized that plans do not always go as expected, so it is important to stay patient and adjust.

Personal Reflection

The fifth day was both fun and relaxing. I enjoyed the time in Tagaytay and the moments I shared with my friends. Although the day did not end as planned, I understood that some situations are beyond control. This experience reminded me to stay positive and make the most of each moment.

Journey to Baguio and New Experiences

April 10, 2026Baguio City

Summary of Activities

At around 1:12 AM, we departed for Baguio by bus, with two stops for bathroom breaks during the trip. We arrived at the Strawberry Farm at around 5:50 AM, where I bought souvenirs such as strawberry jam and wallets. Although the farm was not yet open, we still enjoyed the nearby stalls and tried strawberry taho.

At around 7:15 AM, we visited the La Trinidad Bell Church, where we explored and took pictures, and then we proceeded to Prince Plaza Hotel. DJM assisted us with check-in, and we had breakfast, settled in, and were given time to rest before lunch.

After lunch, we continued our tour to the Philippine Military Academy (PMA), where we explored areas such as Airpower Park, Cavalier’s Park, and the Gregorio del Pilar Statue, and we also saw cadets running in pairs as part of their discipline. We then visited The Mansion and Mines View Park. Although I felt tired at that time, I still bought some souvenirs before we returned to the hotel to rest.

In the evening, after dinner, we went to the Night Market near Burnham Park, where my friends and I bought clothes, ate at food stalls, and tried pedal go-karts, which were tiring but fun. We returned to the hotel before 11:00 PM, and although we were tired, the experience was worth it. I washed up and went to sleep.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned how to manage my energy during long trips and appreciate each destination despite fatigue. I also realized the importance of preparation, especially for long travel hours.

Personal Reflection

The sixth day was both tiring and enjoyable. Despite the long travel time, I appreciated the places we visited and the experiences we had. This day reminded me to make the most of every opportunity, even when I feel tired.

Simple Moments and Last Day in Baguio

April 11, 2026Baguio City

Summary of Activities

We were given time until 12:00 PM to enjoy the surroundings so after breakfast, I went with three friends to Burnham Park. While waiting for the roller skating rink to open, we walked around, took pictures, and bought a few items. We also passed by Maharlika, where I bought ube jam from Good Shepherd. I was unsure at first, but my sister liked it when I brought it home, so it turned out to be a good choice.

When the skating rink finally opened, we decided to try roller skating. None of us knew how to skate, and although we thought it would be easy, it was not. I spent most of the time falling and laughing with other people who were also struggling, which made the experience even more fun. Even if it was difficult, I still enjoyed it. After that, we returned to the hotel feeling tired but happy.

We then prepared our things for checkout, had lunch, and left the hotel. We traveled back to the DJM Dorm by bus, with two stops along the way. At one stop, Mega Station, we bought food and ate on the bus. When we arrived, we settled in, and I went with a friend to a nearby laundry shop. After that, I organized my things for the next day, took a bath, and went to sleep.

◆ ◆ ◆

Key Learnings

I learned that trying new activities can be challenging, but it can still be enjoyable even with mistakes. I also realized the importance of preparing ahead and managing time during travel.

Personal Reflection

The last day in Baguio was simple but meaningful. I enjoyed the time I spent with my friends, especially during roller skating. Even though we struggled, we still had fun together. This day reminded me that even small moments can become memorable experiences.

Journey Back Home

April 12, 2026Zamboanga City

Summary of Activities

We woke up early and had breakfast before preparing for our departure. I was assigned to the second batch for the bus, and we were given packed lunches. Our teacher assisted us with checking in our luggage at the airport.

After that, I waited with the other students for boarding. We arrived early, so we spent some time at the airport and had our lunch while waiting for our flight. At around 2:00 PM, we boarded the plane and traveled back to Zamboanga.

When we arrived, we sent updates and pictures in our group chat. My family picked me up at the airport, and when I got home, I shared the souvenirs that I had bought during the trip.

◆ ◆ ◆

I learned the importance of staying organized and prepared during travel, especially when managing time, luggage, and schedules. I also realized how group coordination helps make the travel process smoother.

Personal Reflection

The final day felt both tiring and fulfilling. I was happy to return home and see my family, but I also felt that the tour ended quickly. This experience gave me new memories, lessons, and motivation that I will carry with me moving forward.
